Engineering Reliability Since 2001

Founded in 2001, AMAANAT MEDICAL DIAGNOSTICS EQUIPMENT LIMITED (previously Amaanat Network Nigeria Ltd.) is a dynamic and indigenous Medical Engineering Company with over two decades of operational excellence.

We specialize in the sales, installation, and maintenance of hi-tech diagnostics and laboratory equipment including CT scanners, X-ray systems, ultrasound machines, surgical instruments, and HLA Tissue Typing/Cross Match equipment. Our services extend across health institutions nationwide, and we have entered into Public-Private Partnerships with private and government health institutions.
